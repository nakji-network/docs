Welcome to Nakji!

Hi! Welcome to Nakji! 🥳

We're happy that you're here. Before we get started, let's talk about what Nakji solves and why Nakji is your choice of data infrastructure platform for your next project.

Nakji is currently being offered at free-of-cost. We’d love to hear about your data needs— to participate in the beta program, please fill out our waitlist form here.​

🤔 What's wrong with today's blockchain data infrastructure?​

In today's Web 3.0, on-chain data is extremely complex and difficult to navigate with speed, making it time consuming and resource intensive to build robust data infrastructure in-house. When surveying dApps, we found that a key pain point in the industry is the lack of easily accessible distributed data in real-time. Today’s existing blockchain data indexing solutions face three main caveats:

  1. Limited data coverage: Not all on-chain data from major blockchains are made available.
  2. Lack of real-time data: Access to on-chain data is often delayed.​
  3. Unreliable uptime: Existing on-chain data providers don’t meet the highest performance requirements.

😮 What does Nakji solve?​

Nakji indexes on and off chain data with a decentralized ecosystem, providing developers with a fast and reliable data instructure.

You can integrate any smart contracts via Nakji connectors and get blockchain data with ease using tools already familiar to you: REST API and Websocket. Nakji's modular system design can be progressively updated to serve the data you need. Please refer to the API section for more technical details.

🤩 Why Nakji?​

Nakji is extremely well-connected, fast, and versatile. Compared to the leading data infrastructure platform today, Nakji is:

  • Up to 20x faster in querying on-chain data (e.g. Uniswap historical transactions)
  • Unlimited historical data query for data pairs
  • Immediate aggregation of real-time data via aggregator connectors
  • Seamless cross-chain data integration with a breadth of on-chain and off-chain connectors
  • Open-source with thorough documentation and source connectors for faster smart contract integration
  • A dedicated team here to support you in the smoothest and most productive onboarding journey

Networks supported by Nakji​

We are also connected to numerous dApps deployed on these networks in DeFi, GameFi, NFT, and more!

If you are looking for a chain that’s not listed here, you can make a connector for the chain as well. We have listed an example of a Nakji connector here. If you are interested in working with us to build out new connectors, please contact us at hello@nakji.network.

🧐 What are some sample use cases?​

Metaverse and GameFi​

  • Seamless cross-chain data accessibility
    • Powered by Nakji’s data agnostic design, pull content from any chain into your metaverse
  • Historical and real time data at your fingertips
    • Track user interactions and content engagements, creating a truly immersive and personalized experience
  • Digital asset and GameFi data at real-time speed
    • Query digital asset data and GameFi protocol securely in milliseconds

NFT​

  • Indexed NFT Metadata from every major chain
    • Render NFT content at lightning speed
  • Real-time updates on NFT transactions
    • Track live NFT prices, transactions and fees
  • Historical data refreshed in milliseconds
    • Access an extensive coverage of historical NFT transaction data with ease and speed

DeFi​

  • No latency in live data
    • Plugged into the freshest, real-time data– trading cannot tolerate even a second of delay
  • Easy access to historical data
    • No more data gap in your DeFi protocol
  • Cross-chain data accessibility in one data infrastructure platform
    • Nakji’s data agnostic and modular design enables you to seamlessly query unlimited multi and cross-chain data

Analytics​

  • Full coverage of real-time activities
    • Freshest alpha signals from candle stick prices, flows, transactions, active wallets, NFT and DeFi metrics, and beyond
  • Unlimited historical data at lightning speed
    • Visualize dynamic and comprehensive data analytics and personalized curation in milliseconds
  • Easily expand your analytics to new chains and protocols
